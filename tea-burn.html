Tea Burn touts a formula rich in natural ingredients known for their weight loss and metabolic benefits. The ingredients used in the formation of Tea Burnare mentioned below:

Green Tea Extract

Green Tea Extract is a staple in weight loss supplements, celebrated for its high concentration of catechins, which are antioxidants that have been shown to enhance metabolism and assist in the breakdown of fats.
2

Caffeine

Caffeine is a well-known stimulant that increases alertness and can boost the metabolic rate. In weight management, it's used to help increase energy expenditure and promote greater fat oxidation.
3

Chromium

Chromium is a trace mineral that plays a vital role in macronutrient metabolism and the regulation of blood sugar levels, which is crucial for maintaining energy levels and reducing food cravings.
4

L-Carnitine

L-Carnitine is an amino acid that is essential for the transport of fatty acids into the mitochondria, where they are burned for energy, thus supporting the body's ability to utilize fat as a fuel source.
5

L-Theanine

L-Theanine, an amino acid found primarily in tea leaves, is known for its ability to promote relaxation without drowsiness. It's included in weight loss formulas for its potential to reduce stress-related eating.
6

Green Coffee Bean Extract

Green Coffee Bean Extract contains chlorogenic acids, which are believed to affect glucose and fat metabolism, potentially reducing the absorption of carbohydrates and thereby assisting in weight management.
