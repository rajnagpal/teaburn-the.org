Green Tea Extract

Green Tea Extract is a staple in weight loss supplements, prized for its high concentration of catechins, particularly epigallocatechin gallate (EGCG), which is believed to enhance metabolic rate and increase fat burning during exercise and at rest.

Caffeine

Caffeine is a powerful stimulant found naturally in coffee, tea, and certain herbs. It's included in Tea Burn for its ability to raise energy levels, improve focus, and contribute to the body's ability to burn fat by boosting metabolism and enhancing exercise performance.

Chromium

Chromium is a trace mineral that plays a significant role in the body's insulin regulation, which can affect food intake and energy production. Its inclusion in Tea Burn is based on its potential to help control blood sugar levels and possibly reduce cravings.
4

L-Carnitine

L-Carnitine is an amino acid derivative that's involved in metabolism, particularly in the transport of fatty acids into the mitochondria where they can be burned for energy, which is why it's valued in weight loss formulas like Tea Burn.
5

L-Theanine

L-Theanine is an amino acid commonly found in tea leaves, known for its ability to promote relaxation without drowsiness. It's thought to impact weight loss by reducing stress and potentially curbing stress-induced snacking.
6

Green Coffee Bean Extract

Green Coffee Bean Extract is made from unroasted coffee beans and contains chlorogenic acid, which is thought to affect how the body handles blood sugar and metabolism, making it a popular ingredient in weight management products.
